Three Steps to Publication

From research question to Lancet-ready manuscript

01

Define Your Study

Select a research phenotype or enter NHANES variable codes. Upload a Word research proposal for AI-powered variable mapping.

02

Analyze Data

Platform downloads data from CDC, applies survey weights, runs regression models, and generates publication-quality figures automatically.

03

Download Paper

Get a complete manuscript with structured abstract, all sections, Lancet-format tables, forest plots, and Vancouver-format references.

Capabilities

Everything You Need

Publication-grade epidemiological analysis

NHANES Data Engine

Automated CDC XPT file download, multi-cycle merging, parquet caching. Supports all cycles from 1999-2020.

Survey-Weighted Analysis

Proper complex survey design with PSU, strata, and weights. Follows NCHS analytic guidelines.

Publication Figures

Forest plots, survival curves, histograms, correlation heatmaps — all 300 DPI, Lancet color palette.

Lancet Tables

Table 1 baseline characteristics, regression results tables, subgroup analyses — all formatted per journal standards.

AI Paper Writing

Structured abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion — generated from your analysis results.

PubMed Integration

Real-time literature search for related NHANES studies. Auto-formatted Vancouver references.
