Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about NHANES to Lancet pricing

What NHANES data cycles are included in the Free plan?

The Free plan includes demo/synthetic NHANES data for testing the platform. Pro and Enterprise plans include all available NHANES cycles from 1999–2023, with automatic updates as new cycles are released.

What statistical methods are available?

Free: descriptive statistics, weighted means/proportions, basic linear regression. Pro: adds logistic regression, Cox proportional hazards, multinomial regression, subgroup analyses, interaction terms, and multiple imputation. Enterprise adds custom statistical pipelines.

Can I generate manuscripts for journals other than The Lancet?

Pro plans generate Lancet-formatted manuscripts with STROBE compliance. Enterprise plans support additional journal templates including JAMA, BMJ, NEJM, and custom formatting for your target journal.

Is NHANES data usage compliant?

NHANES data is publicly available from the CDC/NCHS. The platform follows all NCHS data use guidelines. Survey weights are applied according to NCHS analytical guidelines. Enterprise plans include additional compliance documentation.

Do you offer academic discounts?

Yes! Verified academic institutions receive 30% off Pro and Enterprise plans. Contact us at contact@nhanes-to-lancet.com with your institutional email for verification.
